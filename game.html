GeoFS

GeoFS is an online flight simulator that allows players to experience realistic aircraft flying directly in a web browser. It features real-world maps, different types of aircraft, and basic flight physics. Players can explore the world, practice flying skills, and enjoy a simple yet immersive aviation experience without installing heavy software.

Eaglercraft

Eaglercraft is a browser-based version of Minecraft that lets players play without installing the original game. It features classic Minecraft gameplay such as building, mining, and survival, and can be played on low-end devices through a web browser. It is popular for quick access and multiplayer fun in restricted environments.
